Our story

Built by a founder,
for founders.

UHP didn't start as a business plan. It started as a personal crisis — and the decision to build something better.

Why we exist

Founders deserve better health support.

Most health programs are built for people with stable routines, low stress, and 2 hours free each day. Founders have none of that — and every program they try fails them because of it.

UHP was built on a different premise: if you design the system around actual founder constraints, health becomes simple instead of another thing to fail at.

2016
The breaking point
Steven was building his second company — gaining weight, running on 6 coffees a day, losing focus. Every health solution he tried made things more complicated, not less.
2018
Building the system
After 2 years of research with dietitians, doctors, and sleep specialists, Steven developed a simplified framework that worked with a founder's lifestyle instead of against it.
2020
First clients
Other founders started asking what changed. Steven started coaching 5 founders using the same system. All 5 hit significant results within 12 weeks.
2022
UHP officially launches
The Ultimate Health Project launched with a full coaching program, personalized supplement protocol, and accountability system. 100+ clients in year one.
2026
500+ founders optimized
Today UHP has helped over 500 founders regain their energy, lose fat sustainably, and build health habits that actually last — without sacrificing their business.

FAQ

Common questions.

How is this different from hiring a regular nutritionist?

Regular nutritionists give you a meal plan and see you monthly. UHP is built specifically for founders — we build around your travel schedule, your stress patterns, your work hours, and your actual food environment. The accountability model is also very different: we're available when you need us, not just at scheduled appointments.

How much time does this actually require from me?

The weekly check-in call is 20-30 minutes. Beyond that, it's about applying a simple system that we design around your existing life — not adding a second job. Most clients report the system takes less mental energy than trying to figure it out alone.

Do I need to follow a strict diet or meal plan?

No. Rigid meal plans are exactly what we avoid. The UHP approach uses a flexible nutrition framework that gives you principles and targets — not a list of specific foods. You'll be eating out, traveling, and living normally while still hitting your goals.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice energy improvements within the first 2-3 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically begin around weeks 4-6. Our average client hits significant results (6-10kg fat loss, major energy improvement) by week 12. Results depend on consistency and starting point.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Essentials and Elite programs are month-to-month with no lock-in. The VIP Concierge has a 3-month minimum commitment due to the level of investment on our side. We've never had anyone want to cancel because the results speak for themselves.

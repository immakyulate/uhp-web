Common questions

FREQUENTLY
asked questions.

How quickly will I see results?
Most clients notice energy improvements within 1–2 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically begin at weeks 4–6, with sustainable fat loss compounding from there.
Do I need to track calories or macros?
Not necessarily. We build your approach around what you'll actually maintain. Some clients find tracking useful — others don't. We work that out in the initial consultation.
What does coaching look like day-to-day?
Regular check-in sessions, direct messaging on the Premium program, and a plan updated as your life changes. Structured support at a cadence that works for you — not daily nagging.
I travel constantly. Can this work?
Absolutely — travel is one of the most common challenges we build around. Specific protocols for airports, hotels, and client dinners are baked into your plan from day one.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No lock-in contracts on monthly programs. We believe results speak for themselves — you stay because it's working, not because you're obligated to.

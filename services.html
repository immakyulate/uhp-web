Common questions

Before you
book.

If there's something else on your mind, just ask on the call — no obligation, no sales pressure.

How quickly will I see results?
Most clients notice energy improvements within 1–2 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically begin at weeks 4–6, with sustainable fat loss compounding from there. The system is built for results that stay, not a 4-week spike.
Do I need to track calories or macros?
Not unless you want to. We provide practical nutrition guidance and personalized meal structures that adapt to your schedule. Our focus is on sustainable habits and simple daily anchors rather than macro obsession. Some clients find tracking useful, but it is entirely optional.
I travel constantly. Can this work?
Absolutely — travel is one of the most common challenges we build around from day one. Airport protocols, hotel room options, client dinner strategies — all pre-built into your plan so you don't have to think on the fly.
What does day-to-day coaching look like?
Daily interaction with the UHP Coach assistant — quick check-ins, real-time answers to situations as they come up (a business dinner, a rough sleep, a busy week). Regular 1:1 sessions to review data and adjust the plan. Structured support, not daily nagging.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No lock-in contracts. You stay because it's working — not because you're obligated. Pricing and commitment structures are discussed honestly on the free call, no pressure.
Where are you based? Can we work remotely?
Based in Regents Park, NSW — but the UHP System is fully remote-first. Clients are based across Sydney, interstate, and internationally. All coaching is delivered via Zoom and the UHP Coach platform.

Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What happens in the free health audit?

It's a 30-minute Zoom call with Steven. We review your current eating patterns, sleep, exercise, energy levels, and any supplements you're taking. By the end of the call, you'll have a clear picture of your 3 biggest performance leaks and a practical next step — whether you work with us or not.

How quickly do people typically see results?

Most clients notice a meaningful shift in energy within the first 2 weeks — that's usually from optimising sleep and removing inflammatory foods. By week 4–6, the body composition changes become visible. 12-week results average 8–14 kg of fat loss while preserving lean mass, with 94% reporting significantly better focus.

Do I need to track calories or follow a strict plan?

No. We build flexible systems that work around your life and schedule — not the other way around. Calorie counting is one tool of many, and for most founders it creates more stress than it solves. We focus on food quality, meal timing, and simple principles that don't require obsessive tracking.

I travel constantly and eat out most days. Can this still work?

Absolutely — this is our specialty. We design protocols specifically for high-travel schedules, restaurant eating, jet lag recovery, and inconsistent gym access. Many of our best results have come from clients who travel 3+ weeks per month. We build resilience into the system, not dependence on ideal conditions.

What does ongoing coaching actually look like?

Depending on your plan: weekly check-in calls, direct WhatsApp or Telegram access to Steven for real-time questions, protocol updates as your body adapts, and quarterly biomarker reviews if you choose to test. We're active coaches, not passive advice-givers — expect us to be genuinely engaged in your progress.
